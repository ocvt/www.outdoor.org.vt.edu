Christina McIntyer - Acvisor

Christina McIntyre is the Director of Major Scholarships for the Honors College at Virginia Tech. She is a member of the National Association of Fellowship Advisors (NAFA), and the National Collegiate Honors Council (NCHC) and serves on NCHC's Major Scholarships committee and as a co-chair for NCHC's Partners in the Parks, a collaboration with the National Park Service. Her undergraduate and graduate studies were in nutrition and exercise science. She earned her first Junior Range patch from Yosemite National Park in 1975.
