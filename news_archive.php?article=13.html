OCVT Meeting Monday 9-24 7-8 PM

September 17th, 2012

The next official OCVT meeting will be on Monday the 24th from 7:00-8:00 PM in Squires, Room: 'Brush Mountain A'


For this meeting we will have Mark Wenger the executive director of the ATC come speak to the club. I hope you will all come and give Mark a warm OCVT welcome. As usual we will also have OCVT tshirts available for those of you who have paid online and will be taking dues if you wish to pay in person.
