Missed the first Interest Meeting?

September 18th, 2019

If you were at the first meeting you can ignore this.


Here's a brief recap on what you missed if you couldn't make it to the first meeting:


* If you haven't already - register on the website to receive email notifications 

* Dues for the club aren't mandatory but help gaurentee you a spot on trips

* If you don't see a trip for something you want to do you can lead a trip

* You do not need a car to run a trip - the officer team can help you out

* Trips range in activities (hiking, biking, rockclimbing, trail maintenance) and difficulties - sign up for things that interest you

* EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT THE CLUB IS FOUND ON THE WEBSITE 

* Meetings for the club are sporadic and usually happen on Wednesdays 

Hope to see you on a trip!
