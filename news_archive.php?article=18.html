Welcome Back - Next Meeting Tuesday the 5th 6:00-7:00 in Squires Brush Mountain B

January 28th, 2013

Greetings OCVT, I hope everyone had a nice break from school or work. We’re in the process of gearing up for the new semester and hope to have some great trips out on the OCVT website for you guys soon so keep an eye out for new trips and events.

Our next official meeting will be Tuesday the 5th 6:00-7:00 in Squires Brush Mountain B.

The topic for this meeting is “Cool Gear” if you have any unique interesting or just really cool outdoor gear you want to show the group come bring it to the meeting. It’s OK if you don’t have anything to show, come out and see all the neat stuff that other members bring. For those of you interested in bringing something please email the president with what you are brining so we know how many presenters we will have.
