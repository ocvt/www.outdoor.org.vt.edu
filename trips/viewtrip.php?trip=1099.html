McAfees Knob Dayhike

Trip Leader - Elliot Bevers

Trip Date: Saturday, Oct 3, 2020
1:15 PM - 5:45 PM		 Meeting Date: Saturday, Oct 3, 2020 1:00 PM
Trip Type: Dayhike Max Attendees: 8 People
Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets OK
Drivers Requested: Yes Distance/Difficulty: 8.80 Miles - Moderate


Description

   McAfee Knob is a classic, 4.4 miles in and 4.4 out with about 1,200 feet of elevation gain. Once we get to the top we will stop for abit and take in the view.

Hopefully by starting later in the day we can have better luck with parking.


Location

   The trailhead is about a 40-minute drive from campus unfortunately we cannot carpool right now so make sure you have a ride or you bring your roommate.


Meeting Location

   You must be attending this trip to view this information.


Driving Directions

   


Trip Difficulty

8.80 Miles - Moderate

   The hike up should take about 2 hours with water on the way.


What You Should Bring

   Bring water, lunch, snacks, good hiking shoes, a jacket (if you so choose), and your mask

About halfway there is a water source so bring a filter if you plan on using that
