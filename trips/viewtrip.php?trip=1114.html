Description

   The Virginia's Triple Crown loop is a long 35.1 mile. It includes Dragon's Tooth, McAfee's Knob, and Tinker's Cliff. It will require some training and meetups before we can do it. This will just be an interest meeting via zoom to see if others are interested. If you want to join me after the interest meeting, the meetups will include (trip dates, equipment management and hiking routes). The ballpark dates for the trip are Feb.-Mar., but once we have a group it's up to change. If you are interested in seeing the loop Alltrials.com has the route, reviews, and photos

(https://www.alltrails.com/explore/trail/us/virginia/virginias-triple-crown-loop). If you have any questions or can't make the current meeting please contact me at 7577844619 or email me at clay8894@vt.edu
