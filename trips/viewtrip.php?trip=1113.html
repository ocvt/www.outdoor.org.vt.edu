War Branch Loop Trail

Trip Leader - Clay Mowry

 Meeting Date: 8:45 AM
Trip Type: Dayhike Max Attendees: 4 People

Description

   This loop is rated as moderate, but it’s longer (about 7mi) so you will need the proper clothes, water, etc. it’s said to have two lookouts. The drive is about 40 minutes from VT. Here are the coordinates to the trailhead (37.39048, -80.50820) you can enter that directly into apple/google maps you you should be good. Also I plan on going around 8:45, but if others want to leave at a different time it’s very flexible. The hike should take about 4.5 hours. Don’t be afraid to contact me at (757)784-4619 (I go by Clay).


Trip Difficulty

- Moderate

   Some incline, but difficulty comes from the distance.


What You Should Bring

   Warm clothes, backpack, water, extra water, mask, a snack/lunch, good conversation
