The Signup Period For This Trip Is Over

Annual OCVT Retreat at Mountain Lake

Trip Leader - Doug Atwater

Trip Date: Saturday, Mar 30, 2013
4:00 PM - 12:00 PM		 Meeting Date: 4:00 PM
Trip Type: Special Event Max Attendees: 30 People
Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets NOT Allowed
Drivers Requested: Yes Distance/Difficulty: - None


Description

   Every year OCVT gathers for a retreat at mountain lake. In exchange for trail work they let us camp out the night in the activity barn.

We use this time as fun event to get to know each other and to get away from things for a day. We also use the time to discuss the future of the club and engage people who might want to be more involved in the club next semester.


But either way, casual member or potential officer I encourage you to come out. OCVT will be providing food and weather permitting we will have a campfire.



RIDES: I will be leaving early to lead hikes in the area (see http://www.outdoor.org.vt.edu/trips/viewtrip.php?trip=145)

if you cannot find a ride tell me when you sign up, also indicate if you are spending the night or leaving early. If you can drive someone indicate so when you sign up.


TIME: I will be in the area early, the event starts around 4:00PM we will probably start cooking at 5:00 we need to be out of the barn by noon on Sunday.



Location

   the activity barn is located here http://goo.gl/maps/NjzIm

It is a large open space perfect for our gathering and for setting up sleeping bags and pads. It is heated but usually isn't warm so bring proper clothes.

The barn has bathrooms.


Driving Directions

   http://goo.gl/maps/Ee1qf park in the gravel lot by the barn.


What You Should Bring

   Sleeping Bag and Pad. change of clothes. bring warm clothes if we go outside hat/gloves/scarf bring clothes to be comfortable inside, the barn should be heated to ~50

Bring anything else you want that might be fun.
