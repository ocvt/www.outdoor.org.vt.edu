Orienteering at Heritage Park

Trip Leader - Chris Brumback

Trip Type: Dayhike Max Attendees: 10 People
Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets OK

Description

   So I got a fair number of requests to do another beginner map and compass trip so here it is! This one will be at heritage park. For those who know how to use a map and compass and those who don't, all are welcome! I will provide maps and have a few extra compasses for anyone who needs one. I will be providing instruction for anyone who is new to orienteering or just needs a refresher.


Location

   Heritage Park on Glade Rd.


Meeting Location

   You must be attending this trip to view this information.


What You Should Bring

   If you have a compass please bring it! I will have some extras. Rain gear might be good considering the forecast. A clipboard is also nice if you have it to help you work with the map
