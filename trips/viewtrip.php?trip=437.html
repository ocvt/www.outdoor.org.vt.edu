OCVT Meeting - Trip Leading Social/Training

Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets NOT Allowed

Description

   Let's talks about trips! If you're interested in leading trips this is the meeting for you. We will discuss what makes a good trip as well as tell stories of our past trips.

Even if you're not interested in leading we encourage you to come and hang out and listen to the officers and club members share their experiences.

OCVT will provide some snacks, if the weather improves we'll hold the meeting outside at poverty creek with a nice fire and toast 'mallows!
