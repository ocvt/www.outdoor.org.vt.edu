Orienteering at Ellett Valley Recreational Area

Trip Leader - Chris Brumback

Trip Date: Sunday, Nov 1, 2020
11:00 AM - 1:00 PM		 Meeting Date: Sunday, Nov 1, 2020 11:00 AM
Trip Type: Dayhike Max Attendees: 10 People
Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets OK
Drivers Requested: Yes Distance/Difficulty: - Easy


Description

   Do you ever wake up the day after Halloween and think, "Gosh, I hope I don't get lost in the woods like those people in that horror movie" Well come practice your map and compass skills! Since there are no markers at this park we will just be picking landmarks and navigating to them, so the trip will be pretty laid back. Beginners are welcome! I'll bring extra maps/compasses and will teach any one who is interested!


Location

   Ellet Valley is on the south side of Blacksburg. BT Corporate Research route stops near by so you can get there by bus


Meeting Location

   You must be attending this trip to view this information.


What You Should Bring

   If you have your own compass please bring it. I only have a few. I will be providing maps. Also, there is a chance of rain so bring rain gear.
