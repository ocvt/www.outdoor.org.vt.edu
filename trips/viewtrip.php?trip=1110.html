Groundhog trail - Maintenance

Trip Leader - Colin Wargo

Trip Date: Saturday, Oct 31, 2020
9:55 AM - 2:00 PM		 Meeting Date: Saturday, Oct 31, 2020 9:45 AM
Trip Type: Worktrip Max Attendees: 3 People
Attendee Restrictions: Open To Everyone Pets: Pets NOT Allowed
Drivers Requested: Yes Distance/Difficulty: - Strenuous


Description

   Come out to Groundhog Trail this Saturday, where there's hopefully a downed tree or two to clear off the trail.

Because of the pandemic we have to meet at the trailhead, work in small groups(four people or fewer), and wear masks when working in close proximity of one another.

All trail work related tools and PPE (gloves, safety glasses, helmets) will be provided but you will need to bring your own mask.

The Appalachian Trail Conservancy also requires that you watch the following video and submit the Acknowledgement at the bottom of the video.
https://appalachiantrail.org/get-involved/volunteer/safety/covid-19/

Bring water, and a few snacks for the trail. I plan on finishing up no later than 2, but it could be earlier if there isn't much work to do.
