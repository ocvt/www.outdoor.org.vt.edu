Summer Employment Opportunities with Wilderness Adventure at Eagle Landing

January 14th, 2020

If you're in Blacksburg for the summer and looking for a summer job, check out https://www.wilderness-adventure.com/about/employment! They have positions open for this summer 2020 starting between April-June. It's an adventure camp located 45 minutes away from Blacksburg. If you have any questions feel free to reach out to Nick Wilson, the activities director, at nick@wilderness-adventure.com
