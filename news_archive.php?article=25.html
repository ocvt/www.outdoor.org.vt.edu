OCVT Meeting! 10/8

October 7th, 2013

The next Outdoor Club Meeting will be tomorrow, Tuesday October 8 from 7:30 PM - 8:00 PM in Pamplin 2030. (From now on all meetings will be in Pamplin 2030 from 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM)

We will briefly be having a discussion on trail etiquette and general backpacking and hiking guidelines to have a safe, fun adventure.

After the meeting we will head over to the hiker happy hour, everyone is welcome to join!

As always T-shirts will be available to pick up, or purchase at the meeting.
