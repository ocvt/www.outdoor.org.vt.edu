First Official Meeting - Sept 10th

September 7th, 2012

Mark your calendars everyone, the first official OCVT meeting will be Monday September 10th, 7:00PM - 8:00PM in the GLC auditorium (Google Maps). We will cover the following things at this meeting:

  • Introduction to the club officers.

  • What the club is all about and what activities we do.

  • Our trail maintenance mission.

  • What it means to be a member and what is expected of members.

  • Paying dues and paid membership perks.

  • Services offered by the club.

  • Upcoming trips.


After the meeting we will set up to take dues in person and give out T-shirts. After that we are planning on having a post meeting social gathering so you can get to know your fellow OCVT members. We're working to bring you the best OCVT fall semester yet and hope to see you all at this first meeting.
