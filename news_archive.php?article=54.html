ALDHA Care Spring Clean Up March 31

March 17th, 2018

 Come join the Appalachian Long Distance Hikers Association as we show our appreciation for the Holy Family Hostel in Pearisburg with repairs and clean-up on March 31!

The event will begin at 9am at the Holy Family Hostel in Pearisburg, where we will be giving the Hostel a thorough cleaning, and make repairs to the fence that separates the Hostel and the long term care facility next door. We will also make other repairs as they come up that day.

We will have a dinner provided by ALDHA, and also hand out a few prizes and awards. It's always a great time, and the Holy Family Hostel is more than deserving. ALDHA is an off-trail family of hikers, dreamers and friends of the trail working to preserve, protect and promote the long-distance hiking community.
